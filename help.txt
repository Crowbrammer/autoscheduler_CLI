The Autoscheduler CLI Documentation

Usage examples:

    g <gratitude>         -> Stores gratitude
    g prev                -> Shows the past ten gratitudes
    g prev --amt=<number> -> Shows the past <number> gratitude(s)
    g prev --amt=all      -> Shows all gratitudes
